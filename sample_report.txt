ANNUAL FINANCIAL REPORT - ACME CORPORATION
Fiscal Year 2024

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Acme Corporation reported total revenue of 45.2 million dollars for fiscal year 2024, representing a 12 percent increase over the prior year. Net income reached 6.8 million dollars, driven by improved operational efficiency and cost reduction initiatives.

REVENUE ANALYSIS
The technology solutions division contributed 22 million dollars, representing 49 percent of total revenue. The consulting services division generated 15 million dollars. The remaining 8.2 million came from the maintenance and support division.

DEBT AND LIABILITIES
Total long-term debt stands at 28 million dollars, with a debt-to-equity ratio of 0.85. The company successfully refinanced its senior credit facility, reducing the interest rate from 5.2 percent to 3.8 percent. The company leverage ratio remains within acceptable limits.

RISK FACTORS
Key financial risks include exposure to foreign currency fluctuations. The company faces competitive pressure in the technology market which could impact pricing and margins. Rising interest rates could increase the cost of servicing debt obligations.

CASH FLOW
Operating cash flow was 9.3 million dollars, a significant improvement from 6.1 million in the prior year. Capital expenditures totaled 3.2 million for technology infrastructure upgrades. Free cash flow of 6.1 million provides adequate liquidity for investments.

OUTLOOK
Management expects revenue growth of 8 to 10 percent in fiscal year 2025. The company plans to invest 5 million in research and development. Cost optimization programs are expected to improve operating margins.
